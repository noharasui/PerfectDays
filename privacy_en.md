# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-11-18

This Privacy Policy explains how this application handles user data in accordance with the **Japanese APPI (Act on the Protection of Personal Information)** and the **EU GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)**. This app is a personal diary tool; data is **never shared, sold, or used for advertising**.

---

## 1. Data We Collect
Users may choose whether to provide the following data:

### 1. Images
Used to store diary entries. Images may be saved locally or in cloud services chosen by the user.

### 2. Location Data
Collected through place_picker_google or system APIs, only when the user manually selects a location.

### 3. Health & Activity Data
Such as step count, used only to enhance diary entries.

### 4. Calendar Data
Allows users to link events to diary entries.

### 5. Analytics Data (Firebase Analytics)
Used exclusively for performance analysis and product improvement. All data is anonymized or de-identified.

---

## 2. How We Use Data
We use collected data only for:

- Providing diary-related features  
- Improving app performance and user experience  
- Ensuring security and functionality  
- Anonymous statistical analysis  

**We never use data for advertising, tracking, or sharing with third parties.**

---

## 3. Data Storage & Security
- Data may be stored locally or in user-selected cloud services.  
- Anonymous analytics data may be processed on Firebase servers (including overseas servers).  
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures under APPI & GDPR.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services
The app uses the following services:

### • Firebase Analytics  
Anonymous usage analytics.

### • place_picker_google  
Location selection functionality.

---

## 5. User Rights (GDPR & APPI)
Users have the right to:

- Access their stored data  
- Request correction or deletion  
- Withdraw permissions (location, health data, etc.)  
- Request restriction of processing  
- Export diary data stored locally  

Please contact us through the app’s support feature for such requests.

---

## 6. Data Retention
- Diary and media data are kept until the user deletes them.  
- Analytics retention follows Firebase’s default policies.

---

## 7. Children’s Privacy
This app is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect children’s data.

---

## 8. Policy Updates
This policy may be updated periodically due to feature changes or legal requirements. Updates will take effect immediately upon publication within the app.

---

## 9. Contact
For any questions regarding this policy, please contact us via the in-app support channel.
